About MedPlant

Traditional medicine has relied heavily on medicinal herbs for many years. The effective use of these plants depends on their precise identification. The goal of this project is to create a web-based platform that uses image processing methods to make it easier to identify or recognize medicinal plants automatically. The online interface is been built using the Django framework and other web technologies, and the picture analysis will be performed using Python, Image Processing and Deep Learning Model.

The Problem Statement. It’ll blow your mind.

India is well renowned for its riches in medicinal plants due to its vast floral heritage, yet one of the most pressing problems in Ayurvedic pharmaceuticals is how to identify these plants. Multiple illicit substances are marketed under the same name, which causes confusion and leads to misidentification. Due to seasonal and geographic availability, as well as comparable traits, even collectors and dealers are not totally aware of the precise morphological appearance or differentiating qualities of the many substances. Additionally, the substantial consumption necessary to balance supply and demand places a significant strain on the available resources. Eventually, it results in the practice of adulteration, substitution, and disbelief in the system's power to cure. Thus, software that can recognize many medicinal plants using Image Processing and other Machine Learning Algorithms will be quite useful. It will be beneficial at every stage of the supply chain for the raw materials used in the system, including wholesaler, distributor, etc.

Solving the Problem. It’ll Blow Your Mind.


All across the world, medicinal plants are used in healthcare and herbal treatments. However, incorrect plant identification might have harmful implications on one's health.

This problem is solved by the proposed system, which uses images or photographs to automate the process of plant identification. Users are able to input images of medicinal plants, and the algorithm will correctly identify them.

The sytem returns the desired results to the user which includes the name of the respective plant and the confidence associated with it, that signifies the matching percentage with the actual plant.

Aim of MedPlant

The project's goal is to create a reliable system that uses image processing to automatically identify medicinal plants. This project will offer a user-friendly and precise tool for the identification of medicinal plants by combining Python's image processing capabilities with the Django framework's web building characteristics. The system's ongoing development and community involvement will benefit the healthcare and herbal medicine industries.
